Welcome to Rajasthan Beauty Parlour

The founder of Rajasthan Beauty Parlour and Institute, Mrs. Suwarna Gugle is a trailblazer for beauticians in our country. What started out as sound advice from her mother, awoke a passion for Beauty Therapy she had not foreseen.

To further her qualifications, she is a graduate in Psychology, completed the ‘Cidesco’ exam with an impressive 97% and her demonstration of the different aspects of yoga is kept and watched in Denmark for people who wish to learn Yoga for health. Mrs. Suwarna has since then given many demonstrations and workshops in beauty therapy all over the country. She has conducted seminars and lectures at Rotary Club, Lions Club, Medi Jain Association, Pune radio (?), All India Radio and is an Industry partner at the widely known Symbiosis college.
