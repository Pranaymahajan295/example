Our Story

Founded in 1964 by Bhikaram Chandra Jalan, Haldiram's has grown from a small sweet shop in Nagpur to one of India's most recognized and trusted food brands. We are committed to providing authentic Indian snacks and sweets that capture the true essence of Indian flavors.

Our journey has been one of constant innovation, quality improvement, and expansion. Today, Haldiram's products are enjoyed by millions across India and around the world.

  • Quality ingredients sourced from the best suppliers
  • Traditional recipes with a modern twist
  • Rigorous quality control at every stageli>
  • Sustainable and ethical business practices
  • Commitment to customer satisfaction
