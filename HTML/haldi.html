Our Legacy

Founded in 1937 by Ganga Bishan Agarwal, Haldiram's has grown from a small shop in Bikaner to become India's leading manufacturer of traditional Indian snacks and sweets. Our commitment to quality, authenticity, and taste has made us a household name across the globe.

With over 80 years of experience, we continue to innovate while preserving traditional recipes passed down through generations. Every product is crafted with the finest ingredients and utmost care.
